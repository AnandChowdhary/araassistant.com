Languages

Ara supports over 100 languages and uses neural machine translation to send your emails in your recipient’s language.

Timezones

Ara understands and supports all of the world’s timezones, so you can tell Ara where your recipient is and she’ll talk to them in their locale.

Calendars

Connect Ara to your Google Calendar and she’ll intelligently schedule your appointments for when you’re free within your scheduling hours.

Templates

Create templates for common emails (like your payment details or address) and Ara will automatically share them with your recipients.

Locations

Ara will remember your preferred meeting locations and you can set defaults for coffee, dinner, Skype, and more.

Chatbot

Ara can be set up as a chatbot on your website to capture leads and answer frequently asked questions automatically.

Developers

Ara comes with an open RESTful API for developers. Build your apps to set up meetings, perform tasks, and more.
